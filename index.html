Philosophy

Why Film?

Focus & Control

My photographs are made with traditional film cameras. Shooting classic, manual cameras allows me slow down and create each photograph with purpose.

Tangible Process

Film photography gives me tangible control of my work. From loading the film to developing it at the lab, the process is hands-on and timeless.

From Negative to Digital

After developing your film, I scan each roll frame-by-frame so that you receive analog images in convenient digital format — ready to print and share with loved ones.

Documentary Approach

My photographs are made honestly while observing relationships and real moments. Your photos will tell a visual story driven by genuine interaction and emotion.

About

The Photographer

Billy Grubbs is a Louisville-based documentary photographer specializing in analog film. With over 150 weddings photographed since 2007, Billy brings a quiet, observational presence to every shoot.

Billy is also the founder of State Film Lab in Louisville, developing film for photographers across the nation. This deep connection to the craft shapes every image he makes.

He is available for weddings, portraits, and commercial work in Kentucky and abroad.
